..where everyone is happy!

It's a happy, sunny day!
Why don't we all come out and play?
Why don't we all shout hooray?
Cause it's a happy, sunny day!
Come join us and we'll have fun
Where we can play in the sun
And skip, and hop, and dance, and run
It's Happy Land, the place of fun!
